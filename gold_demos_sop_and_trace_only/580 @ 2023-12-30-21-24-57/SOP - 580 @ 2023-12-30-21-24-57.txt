Create a new forum named sci_fi, with a description of A wild place for sci-fi enthusiasts, and include ['New', 'Classic', 'Movies', 'Post my novel', 'Random'] in the sidebar.
1. Click on the white "Forums" option in the top menu, right to the "Postmill" option.
2. Click on the red "Create forum" button at the top right of the screen.
3. Type "sci_fi" in the "Name" text box under the "Create new forum" title.
4. Type "sci_fi" in the "Title" text box under the "Name" text box.
5. Type "A wild place for sci-fi enthusiasts" in the "Description" text box under the "Title" text box.
6. Type "New Classic Movies Post my novel Random" in the "Sidebar" text box under the "Description" text box.
7. Click on the red "Create forum" button at the bottom left of the screen.
