Show me the name of the customers who have expressed dissatisfaction with Chloe tank
1. Click on the "Reports" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "By Products" under the "Reviews" section in the bottom left.
3. Click in the empty textbox at the top of the 2nd column to filter by "Product".
4. Type "chloe" to search for relevant reviews.
5. Press the "Enter/Return" key to execute the search.
6. Click the blue "Show Reviews" on the right of the page.
7. Given the information, state the customers who have expressed dissatisfaction with Chloe tank.
