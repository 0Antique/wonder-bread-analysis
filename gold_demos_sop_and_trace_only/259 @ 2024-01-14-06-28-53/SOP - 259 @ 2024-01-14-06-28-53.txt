Get me my RSS feed token
1. Click the my account button, which is an icon of the user’s profile picture, and is the leftmost icon in the purple navigation area near the top of the page.
2. Click on “Edit profile” in the pop-up menu.
3. Click on “Access Tokens” in the sidebar.
4. Scroll down to the “Feed token” section.
5. Click the eye icon, to the right of the feed token text.
6. Return the RSS feed token. This is the text in the box under the small bolded text “Feed token”.
