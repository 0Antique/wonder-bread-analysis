Create a private Android repository called "web_agent_android" using the right template to speed up development.
1. Locate and click on the blue "New Project" button on the top right side of the page
2. Click on the "Create from template" icon and click on the "Android" template from the list
3. Click on the "Project name" textbox and type in "web_agent_android"
4. Under the "Visibility Level" section, click the "Private" box
5. Click the blue "Create project" button
