Presents the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Orders' under 'Sales' in the popup window.
3. Click on the dropdown box next to 'Period'.
4. Click on 'Month' in the dropdown list.
5. Type '5/1/22' in the 'From' text box.
6. Type '12/31/22' in the 'To' text box.
7. Click on the dropdown box next to 'Order Status'.
8. Click on 'Specified' in the dropdown list.
9. Click on 'Completed' from the list of order status types.
10. Click on the 'Show Report' orange button at the top right.
11. Scroll down to see the table showing the monthly counts of successful orders.
