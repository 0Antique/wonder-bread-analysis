Add a simple product named Energy-Bulk Man Yoga Pant with 50 in stock, available in size 38 and color yellow, priced at $69.99
1. From the left menu, click on "Catalogue".
2. From the popped up submenu, click on "Products".
3. On the top right of the page, click on the orage "Add Product" button.
4. Enter "Energy-Bulk Man Yoga Pant" in the "Product Name" field.
5. Enter "69.99" in the "Price" field.
6. Scroll down to the bottom of the page, click the grey "Create Configurations" button.
7. Click on the "color" checkbox.
8. Click on the "size" checkbox.
9. Click on the orage "Next" button at the top right of the page.
10. Click on the "yellow" checkbox in the "color" section.
11. Click on the "38" checkbox in the "size" section.
12. Click on the orage "Next" button at the top right of the page.
13. On the bottom of the page, click on the "Apply single quantity to each SKUs" button.
14. Enter "50" in the "Quantity" field that pops up.
15. Click on the orage "Next" button at the top right of the page.
16. Click on the orage "Generate Products" button at the top right of the page.
17. Click on the orage "Save" button at the top right of the page.
