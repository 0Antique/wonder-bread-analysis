Make the LICENSE of byteblaze/a11y-syntax-highlighting to one that mandates all copies and derivative works to be under the same license
1. In the top left purple search bar, search for the keyword "a11y-syntax-highlighting".
2. Click on the first project in the dropdown with a matching name.
3. Scroll down on this page until a LICENSE.txt file becomes visible.
4. Click on the LICENSE file.
5. Click on the blue dropdown menu right to the "Edit/Open in Web IDE" button.
6. Click on the blue "Edit" option.
7. Click on the blue "Edit" button left to the dropdown menu.
8. Click on the "Apply a template" dropdown menu.
9. Type "general" in the "Filter" input box.
10. Under "Popular", select the matching license name.
11. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
12. Click on the blue "Commit changes" button at the bottom of the page.
