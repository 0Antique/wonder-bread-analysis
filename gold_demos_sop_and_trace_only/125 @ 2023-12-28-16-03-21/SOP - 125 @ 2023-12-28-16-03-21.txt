What is the price range of teeth grinding mouth guard in the One Stop Market?
1. Click on "Advanced Search" on the top header of the page.
2. In the "Product Name" field, enter "mouth guard".
3. In the "Description" field, enter "teeth grinding".
4. Click on the "Search" button.
5. Click on the "Sort by" dropdown and select "Price".
6. The Price of the first product will be the lower end of the range.
7. Click on the up arrow located to the right of the sort by box to make it into a down arrow.
8. Now, the price of the first product will be the upper end of the range.
9. From the two prices, report the range.
