To show the most recent cancelled order, do the following.
1. Click on the blue "My Account" button on the top right of the page.
2. On the gray sidebar on the left of the page, click on the "My Orders" button.
3. Look under the "Status" column and find the first row with "Cancelled" in this column.
4. For that row, click on the "View Order" blue button (under the Action column).
