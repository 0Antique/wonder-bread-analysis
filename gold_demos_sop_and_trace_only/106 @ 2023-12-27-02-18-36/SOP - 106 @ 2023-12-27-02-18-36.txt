Display the list of issues in the umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel repository that have labels related to BUG
1. From the tabs under "Projects", click on "Explore".
2. Scroll down until you see "umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel" repository.
3. Click on "umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel" repository from the displayed list of projects.
4. From the left bar, click on "Isuues".
5. In the search bar, type "BUG".
6. Click on the "search" icon next to the search field.
