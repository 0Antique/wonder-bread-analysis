I recently moved, my address is 231 Willow Way, Suite 100, Chicago, IL, 60601, update my information on OneStopShopping accordingly
1. At the top of the screen, click the "My Account" button, written in blue text
2. Next, locate the "Address Book" section at the middle of the screen and click the blue "Edit Address" button at the bottom
3. Click the "Street Address" textbox and type in "231 Willow Way"
4. Click the textbox below it and type in "Suite 100"
5. Click the "State/Province" textbox and locate and click on "Illinois"
6. Click the "City" textbox and type in "Chicago"
7. Click the "Zip/Postal Code" textbox and type in "60601"
