Create a discussion post about "Harry Potter movie series" in a relevant subreddit and ask users for their opinions with the simple prompt, "your opinion"
1. Click on the white "Forums" option in the top menu, right to the "Postmill" option.
2. Click on the gray "Alphabetical" option in the top menu, under the red bar at the top of the screen.
3. Click on the red “movie” link in the second column, under the “M” section.
4. Click on the red "+ Submit" button at the top of the screen, right to the "email" icon.
5. Type "Harry Potter movie series" in the "Title" text box under the "URL" text box.
6. Type “your opinion” in the “Body” text box under “Title”.
7. Click on the red "Create submission" button under the "Forum" dropdown menu.
