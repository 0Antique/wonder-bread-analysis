Get the total payment amount of the last 5 completed orders
1. Click the "Sales" icon from the left sidebar to open the dropdown menu.
2. From the dropdown, click on the first option "Orders".
3. Click on the gray "Filters" button on the top right.
4. Click the down arrow on the dropdown menu for "Status".
5. From the dropdown, click on "Complete".
6. Click the "Apply Filters" button below all the filter options at the right.
7. Click on the fourth column header to sort by "Purchase Date".
8. Click on the column header "Purchase Date" again to reverse the ordering.
9. Given the information, state the the total payment amount of the last 5 completed orders.
