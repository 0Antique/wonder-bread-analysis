Based on the screenshots provided, here is a step-by-step process (SOP) for how to find the total count of not approved reviews amongst all reviews:

1. Click on the “MARKETING” button in the dark gray column on the left hand side of the page.
2. Click on “All Reviews” in the pop up menu, located under the heading “User Content”.
3. The number of pending reviews is the number of records found, which is located directly above the column heading “Created”.
4. Click on the box located directly under the column heading “Status”.
5. Click “Not Approved” in the pop up menu.
6. Press enter.
7. The number of approved reviews is the number of records found, which is located directly above the column heading “Created”.
