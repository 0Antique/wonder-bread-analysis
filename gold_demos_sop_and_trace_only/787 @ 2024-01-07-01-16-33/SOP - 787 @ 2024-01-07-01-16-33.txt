Tell me the number of followers of the contributor who has the most commits to branch main:
1. Open the GitLab main repository page in your web browser.
2. On the left-hand side. click on "Repository" on the sidebar to expand the dropdown if it's not already expanded.
3. Under the "Repository" section, click on "Contributors." This will take you to a page that lists all the contributors to the repository along with their commit counts.
4. Once on the "Contributors" page, click on the dropdown menu next to the “history” button where you can select the branch of interest. 
5. Scroll down to until seeing the branch “master”. Click on “master” branch.
6. You will see a list of contributors sorted by the number of commits they have made. The contributor with the most commits will be listed at the top. 
7. Locate the contributor with the highest number of commits. Their username will be displayed along with the commit count. In this case it is Henning Leutz.
8. Type the user name from the search bar at the top of the screen and press enter. 
9. Click on the user profile.
10. Find the number of followers on the page below the user name. It says “0 followers” in this case.
