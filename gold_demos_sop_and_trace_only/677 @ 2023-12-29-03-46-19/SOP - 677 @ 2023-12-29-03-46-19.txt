Lookup orders that are processing
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Sales" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Click on the "Orders" button to reveal the "Orders" page
4. Click on the gray "Filters" button on the right side of the page and locate the "Status" dropdown menu on the bottom right
5. Click on the "Status" dropdown and click on the "Processing" category
6. Click on the dark gray "Apply Filters" button 
7. Scroll down and use the information provided to answer the question
