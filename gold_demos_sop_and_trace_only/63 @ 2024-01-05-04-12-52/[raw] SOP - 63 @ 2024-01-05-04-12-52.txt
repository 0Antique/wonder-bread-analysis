Which customer(s) has completed the second most number of orders in the entire history?
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Reports" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Click on the "Order Total" button in the "Customers" section of the pop-out menu, to reveal the "Order Total Report" page
4. Click on the "From" textbox and type in the first day of our timeframe: 01/01/2021
6. Click on the "To" textbox and type in the last day of our timeframe: 01/05/2024
7. Click on the dropdown menu labelled "Show By:" and select "Year"
8. Click on the "Refresh" button in gray to display the data
9. Scroll down and use the information provided to calculate which customer has completed the second most number of orders in the entire history
