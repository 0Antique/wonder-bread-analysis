To set the homepage URL on the GitLab profile to "a11yproject.contributor.me", do the following:
1. Click the profile picture icon at very the top right of the screen.
2. On the drop down, click "Edit profile".
3. Scroll until you reach a "Website url" input box (below the Twitter box and above the Location box).
4. Type in "a11yproject.contributor.me" into this "Website url" input box.
5. Scroll down to the bottom of the page.
6. Click on the blue "Update profile settings" button at the bottom of the page.
