How much I spend each month from Jan to the end of March 2023 on shopping at One Stop Market?
1. Click on the blue "My Account" button on the top right of the page.
2. On the left gray sidebar menu, click on "My Orders".
3. Sum up the values under "Order Total" for the rows with a value under the "Date" column that falls between January and March 2023.
4. Click on the right arrow at the bottom of the page to see more orders.
5. Sum up the values under "Order Total" for the rows with a value under the "Date" column that falls between January and March 2023.
6. Add the values you obtained in steps 3 and 5 to get the final answer.
