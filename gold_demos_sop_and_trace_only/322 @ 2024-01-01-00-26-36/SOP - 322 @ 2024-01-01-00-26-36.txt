How much refund I should expect from my order canlled in May 2023 if I cannot get the shipping fee refunded?
1. Click on the blue "My Account" button on the top right of the page.
2. On the left gray sidebar menu, click on "My Orders".
3. Click on the blue "View Order" button for the row with an order date "5/17/23"
4. Scroll down until you see "Grand Total".
5. If you cannot get the shipping fee refunded, you should expect to get the number to the right of "Subtotal" as a refund.
