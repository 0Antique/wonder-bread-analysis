What is the total count of Not Approved reviews amongst all the reviews?
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Marketing" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Click on the "All Reviews" button in the "User Content" section of the pop-out menu, to reveal the "Reviews" page
4. Locate the column called "Status", and find its corresponding dropdown menu below it
5. Click on the texbox and select our keyword: "Not Approved"
6. Press "Enter" on the keyboard to apply the filter to the data
7. Scroll down to find the report and locate the correct information to answer the question
