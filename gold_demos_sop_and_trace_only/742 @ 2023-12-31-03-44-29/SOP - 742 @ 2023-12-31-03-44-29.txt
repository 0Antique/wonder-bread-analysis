Create a new private project "planner" and add Abishek, Vinta as members
1. Locate and click on the blue "New Project" button on the top right side of the page
2. Click on the "Create a blank project" icon
3. Click on the "Project name" textbox and type in "planner"
4. Under the "Visibility Level" section, click the "Private" box
5. Click the blue "Create project" button
6. Click on the "Project Information" section on the gray sidebar
7. Click on "Members"
8. In the upper right side, click the blue "Invite members" button, and in the "Username or email addresses" textbox, type in and click on "Abishek" and "Vinta"
9. Click on the blue "Invite" button
