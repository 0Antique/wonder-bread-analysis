Tell me the count of comments that have received more downvotes than upvotes for the user who made the latest post on the DIY forum.
1. Click on the 'Forums' tab in the top bar.
2. Click on the 'Alphabetical' tab to see all forums.
3. Click on 'DIY'.
4. Click on the dropdown box next to 'Comments'.
5. Click on 'New' from the dropdown list to get the latest post first.
6. Click on the username of the author of the first post which is next to 'Submitted by'.
7. Click on the 'Comments' tab to see comments by this user.
8. Scroll down to count the number of comments with negative total votes.
9. Report the total number of comments with negative total votes.
