Change the page title of "Privacy Policy" page on my site to "No privacy policy is needed is this dystopian world". 
1. Click the brown "Content" tab on the left side of the screen with the postboard icon, underneath the "Marketing" tab
2. Click the brown "Pages" tab in the pop-up to the right of the "Sales" tab and above the "Blocks" tab
3. Click the gray item titled “Privacy Policy” in the list in the table at the bottom half of the screen
4. Click the first textbox in the emerging pop-up to the right of the ID number in the ID column
5. Backspace until the textbox is empty
6. Type "No privacy policy is needed is this dystopian world" 
7. Click the orange "Save" button box to the right of the blue "Cancel" button underneath the selected page item
