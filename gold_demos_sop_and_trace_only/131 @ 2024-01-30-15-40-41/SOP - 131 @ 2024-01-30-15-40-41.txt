What's the total number of items sold in the most recent 7 orders?
1. Click the "Sales" icon from the gray sidebar on the left of the screen.
2. From the sidemenu, click on "Orders", which is the first option.
3. Click on the title of the fourth column "Purchase Date" to sort in Ascending order.
4. Click on the title "Purchase Date" again to reverse the sorting order from most recent to least recent.
5. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 1rst row from top.
6. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
7. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
8. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 2nd row from top.
9. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
10. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
11. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 3rd row from top.
12. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
13. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
14. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 4th row from top.
15. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
16. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
17. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 5th row from top.
18. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
19. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
20. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the sixth row from top.
21. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
22. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
23. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the 7th row from top.
24. Scroll down to see the number of items in this order.
25. Given the information, sum the total number of items from all 7 orders.
