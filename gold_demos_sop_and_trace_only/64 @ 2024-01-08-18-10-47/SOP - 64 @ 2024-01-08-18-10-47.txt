Which customer has placed 2 orders in the entire history?
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Order Count' under 'Customers'.
3. Enter the date '1/1/2015' in the 'From' text box.
4. Enter the date '1/2/2024' in the 'To' text box.
5. Click on the dropdown button next to 'Show by'.
6. Click on 'Year' from the dropdown list.
7. Click on the 'Refresh' button.
8. Scroll down to count the number of orders made by each user.
9. Report the users who have placed exactly 2 orders.
