To Preview the Magento Blank theme for my shop yourself:
1. Click on the "Content" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "Themes" under the "Design" section at the bottom of the menu.
3. Click on the blue "View" button on the right of the row corresponding to "Magento Blank".
4. Click on the "Theme Preview Image" thumbnail (to the right of the text).
