Open the thread of a trending post on the forum "space" and subscribe.
1. Click the red “Forums” tab at the top in white text
2. Click the gray “Alphabetical” tab at the top
3. Scroll until the red “space” text in the “All Forums” section is visible
4. Click the red “space” text in the “All Forums” section
5. Click the gray ‘subscribe’ button on the top right
