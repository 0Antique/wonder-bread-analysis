Add a simple product named Energy-Bulk Man Yoga Pant with 50 in stock, available in size 38 and color yellow, priced at $69.99
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Catalog" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Click on the "Products" button to reveal the "Products" page
4. Locate and click on the orange "Add Product" button on the top right of the page
5. Identify and click on the "Product Name" textbox and type in "Energy-Bulk Man Yoga Pant"
6. Identify and click on the "Price" textbox and type in "69.99"
7. Scroll down and locate the "Configurations" section and click on the dark gray "Create Configurations" button on the right side
8. Click on the "color" and "size" checkboxes to mark them as customizable configurations and click the orange "Next" arrow on the top right
9. In the "Color" section, click "yellow" and in the "Size" section, click "38" and click the orange "Next" arrow on the top right
10. In the "Quantity" section, click "Apply single quantity to each SKUs" and type in "50" in the "Quantity" textbox and click the orange "Next" arrow on the top right
11. Click the orange "Generate Products" button on the top right and click on the orange "Save" button in the top right
