To Post my question, "is car necessary in nyc?", in a subreddit where I'm likely to get an answer yourself, do this:
1. In the header, click "forums"
2. Under that, click the button that says "Alphabetical" to see all the subreddit options.
3. Click on a relevant forum. In this case, I chose "nyc"
4. In the header, click the plus button that says "submit".
5. In the textbox under the "title" header, enter a title that asks your question.
6. At the bottom of the page, press the "Create submission" button.
