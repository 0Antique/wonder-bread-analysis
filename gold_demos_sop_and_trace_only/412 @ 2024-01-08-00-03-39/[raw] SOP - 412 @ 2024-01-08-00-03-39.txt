To Make the LICENSE of byteblaze/accessible-html-content-patterns to Apache License yourself:
1. Search in the top "Search Gitlab" searchbar for "accessible".
2. Click on the "accessible-html-content-patterns" project.
3. Click on the LICENSE.txt file.
4. Click on the blue dropdown arrow on the right of the "Edit/Open in Web IDE" button.
5. Select the "Edit" option (this is white and in the dropdown menu).
6. Click on the blue "Edit" button to the left of the dropdown menu.
7. Click on the "Apply a template" dropdown menu.
8. Select the "Apache License" option under "Popular".
9. Click on the blue "Commit changes" button.
