Display the list of issues in the umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel repository that have labels related to BUG
1. Click into the search bar located at the top left of the page, with a purple background.
2. Type in the name of the desired repository, using the project name following the “/” character.
3. Press enter.
4. Click on the name of the relevant search result, i.e. the name of the desired repository.
5. Click on “Issues” in the sidebar, located on the left side of the page.
6. Click into the search bar, located above the rows of search results.
7. Click on “Label” in the pop-up menu.
8. Click on “=” in the pop-up menu.
9. Type in the key word(s) of the desired label.
10. Click on the relevant label.
11. Press enter.
