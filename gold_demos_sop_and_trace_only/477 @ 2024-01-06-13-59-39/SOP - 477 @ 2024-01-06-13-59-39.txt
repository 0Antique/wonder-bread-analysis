Set up a new, empty repository with the name awesome_program_aided_reasoning?
1. Click on the "plus" button with a plus sign at the top of the screen, in the top header.
2. Click on the "New project/repository" in the dropdown menu that appears.
3. Click on "Create blank project" at the left of the screen.
4. There is a textbox under the text “Project name” and it has filler text “My awesome project.” Type the desired project name in the textbox.
5. Click on the blue "Create project" at the bottom of the screen.
