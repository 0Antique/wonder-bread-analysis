What is the top-1 best-selling product in 2022
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Bestsellers' under 'Products'.
3. Click on the dropdown button next to 'Period'.
4. Click on 'Year' from the dropdown list.
5. Click on the calendar icon next to 'From'.
6. Click on the year dropdown button, next to '2024'.
7. Click on '2022' as the year from the dropdown list.
8. Click on '1' in the calendar to set the start date as '1/1/2022'.
9. Click on the calendar icon next to 'To'.
10. Click on the year dropdown button, next to '2024'.
11. Click on '2022' as the year from the dropdown list.
12. Click on the month dropdown button, next to 'Jan'.
13. Click on 'Dec' as the month from the dropdown list.
14. Click on '31' in the calendar to set the to date as '12/31/2022'.
15. Click on the 'Show Report' orange button on the top right.
16. The first product shown in the resulting table has the highest sales, hence report that product, i.e. Quest Lumaflex Band.
