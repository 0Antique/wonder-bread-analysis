Give me the brand of the products that have 3 units left
1. Click on 'CATALOG' on the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Products' in the popup window.
3. Click on the 'Filter' button on the right side.
4. Type 3 in the 'from' text box under Quantity.
5. Type 3 in the 'to' text box under Quantity.
6. Click the 'Apply Filters' button to search for products that have 3 units left.
7. Scroll down and report brands of all shown products.
