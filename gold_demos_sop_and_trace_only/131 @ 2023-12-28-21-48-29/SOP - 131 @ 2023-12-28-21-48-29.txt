What's the total number of items sold in the most recent 7 orders?
1. On the left menu of the page, click on "Sales".
2. From the popped up sub menu, click on "Orders".
3. On the displayed table, click on the header of the "Purchase Date" column, until an up arrow appears to sort in order of most recent.
4. On the first row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the first order.
5. Count the number of items ordered.
6. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
7. On the second row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the second order.
8. Count the number of items ordered.
9. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
10. On the third row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the third order.
11. Count the number of items ordered.
12. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
13. On the fourth row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the fourth order.
14. Count the number of items ordered.
15. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
16. On the fifth row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the fifth order.
17. Count the number of items ordered.
18. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
19. On the sixth row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the sixth order.
20. Count the number of items ordered.
21. Click on the "back" button on the top bar to view the order list again.
22. On the seventh row, click on "View" under the "Action" column to view the seventh order.
23. Count the number of items ordered.
24. Report the total number of items sold in the most recent 7 orders.
