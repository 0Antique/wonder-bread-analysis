Give me the brand of the products that have 3 units left
1. On the left menu, click on "Catalog".
2. From the popped up sub menu, click on "Products".
3. In the top right corner, click on the "Filters" button.
4. Under the "Quantity" sub heading, enter 3 in the 'From' and 'To' box field.
5. In the bottom right corner, click on "Apply Filters".
6. From the displayed table, report the name of the products that have 3 units left.
