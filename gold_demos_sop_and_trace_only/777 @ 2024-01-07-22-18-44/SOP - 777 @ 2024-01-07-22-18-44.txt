Reduce the price of green Hollister backyard sweater in all size by $5.
1. Click on the "CATALOG" option in the brown left side bar under the "Sales" option.
2. Click on the "Products" option in the "Catalog" menu shown.
3. Search for “Hollister backyard in the "Search by keyword" white search bar.
4. Click on the blue "Edit" link in the "Action" column of the table shown corresponding to the product id 126 and the type “Configurable Product”.
5. Scroll down to first check the price of all colors, and confirm it is all 52.
6.  Scroll up until you see the brown “Edit Configurations" button at the right of the screen.
7. Click on the brown “Edit Configurations" button at the right of the screen.
8. On the screen of “Step 1: Select Attributes”, click on the orange "Next" button at the top right of the screen.
9. On the screen of “Step 2: Attribute Values”, click on the orange "Next" button at the top right of the screen.
10. On the screen of “Step 3: Bulk Images, Price and Quantity”, scroll down to click on the "Apply unique prices to each SKU" option in the “Price” section at the bottom of the screen.
11. Select “Color” in the “Select attribute” dropdown menu.
12. Scroll down and type “47” in the text box for “Green”
13. Type “52” in the text box for “Red”
14. Type “52” in the text box for “White”
15. Click on the orange "Next" button at the top right of the screen.
16. Click on the orange "Generate Products" button at the top right of the screen.
17. Click on the orange "Save" button at the top right of the screen.
