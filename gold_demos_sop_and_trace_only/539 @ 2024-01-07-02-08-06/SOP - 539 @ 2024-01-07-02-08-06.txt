Modify the address of order #65 to 789 Pine Lane, San Francisco, CA, 94102
1. Click on "Sales" in the left sidebar
2. Click "Orders" on the popover options
3. Clear any existing filters if "x" are visible below the "Search by keyword" text box.
4. Click on "Filters"
5. Click on the text box under "ID"
6. Type "65"
7. Press Enter
8. Click on "View" in the first result's row
9. Click the blue "Edit" button next to "Billing Address"
10. Scroll down until the "Street Address" is visible
11. Click the text box next to "Street Address"
12. Clear the contents of the text box, if any
13. Type "789 Pine Lane"
14. Click the dropdown next to "State/Province"
15. Select "California" from the dropdown
16. Click the text box next to "City"
17. Clear the contents of the text box, if any
18. Type "San Francisco"
19. Click the text box next to "Zip/Postal Code"
20. Clear the contents of the text box, if any
21. Type "94102"
22. Click the orange "Save Order Address" in the upper right of the screen
