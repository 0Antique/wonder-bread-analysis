Modify the address of order #65 to 789 Pine Lane, San Francisco, CA, 94102
1. Click the "Sales" tab on the left side of the screen with the dollar sign icon underneath the "Dashboard" tab
2. Click the "Orders" tab at the top of the opening tab bar on the top right that says "Orders", still on the left side of the screen 
3. Click the search box above the list of Orders
4. Backspace until the search bar is empty
5. Type “000000065”
6. Press the enter key
7. Click the first item that shows
8. Click the blue “Edit” link next to the Bolded “Billing Address” title
9. Scroll down until the bold “Street Address” and “Zip/Postal Code” are available
10. Click the text box to the right of the”Street Address *”
11. Backspace until the field is empty
12. Type “789 Pine Lane”
13. Click the “State/Province*” dropdown
14. Scroll until “California” is visible
15. Click “California”
16. Click the text box next to “City*”
17. Backspace until the field is empty
18. Type “San Francisco”
19. Click the text box next to “Zip/Postal Code*”
20. Backspace until the field is empty
21. Type “94102” 
22. On the top right click the orange button that says “Save Order Address”
