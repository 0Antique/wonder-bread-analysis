Buy the best rating product from "Home Audio Speaker" category with at least 5 reviews and the product is least expensive
1. Click on "Electronics" on the second row of the Grey top bar
2. Click on shopping category "Home audio" on the left
3. Click on subsection "speakers"
4. On the right dropdown sort by "Price"
5. Page through the listings and select the first speaker with 5 reviews
6. Click on "add to cart"
7. Select shipping method and payment method
8. Click on "Place order"
