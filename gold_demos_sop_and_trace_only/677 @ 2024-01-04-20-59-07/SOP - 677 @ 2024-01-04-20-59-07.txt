Lookup orders that are processing
1. Click the gray “Sales” tab on the left with the dollar sign above it, above the catalog tab
2. Click the “Orders” tab from the popout on the right, still on the left hand side of the screen at the top
3. Click the gray “Filters” button on the middle right of the screen
4. Click the gray status dropdown on the right side of the screen with the black text
5. Click “Processing” from the dropdown
6. Click the gray “Apply filters” button on the bottom right next to the blue text
