Update the description of Radiant Tee to highlight the real user positive reviews by quoting the comments
1. Click “Marketing” in the left sidebar
2. Click “All Reviews” in the popover options
3. Click the text box under the Product header
4. Clear the text box, if it isn't already
5. Type “radiant"
6. Click on the first positive-sounding review based on the "Review" preview
7. Select all the text in the text box next to "Review"
8. Copy the text
9. Click the "Radiant Tee" button next to "Product"
10. Scroll down until "Content" is visible
11. Click on "Content"
12. Click on the text box under to "Short Description"
13. Paste the text copied earlier with quotation marks around it.
14. Click the orange "Save" button in the upper right of the screen
