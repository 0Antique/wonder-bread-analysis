Open my latest updated issue that has keyword "homepage content" in its title to check if it is closed
1. On the top bar, click on the icon which the coffee mug on it called "Issues".
2. Click the third tab under Issues labeled "All".
3. Click the search box at the top of the page.
4. Enter "homepage content" in the search box.
5. From the popup, click on "Search for this word".
6. On the top right corner of the list, click on the dropwdown.
7. Click on "Updated Date".
8. From the displayed list, click on the first title of the issue which has "homepage content" in its title to check if it is closed.
