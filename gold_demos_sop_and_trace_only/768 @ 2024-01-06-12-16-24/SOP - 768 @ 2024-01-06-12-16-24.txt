5 blue Cronus yoga pants with size 33 arrived, update the stock
1. On the left menu, click on "Catalogue".
2. From the popped up sub-menu, click on "Products".
3. At the top of the displayed table, click on the search field.
4. Enter "Cronus yoga pants".
5. Click on the search icon to the right of the search field.
6. Click on the "edit" section for the product row with "33" and "blue" in the name.
7. Click on the "Quantity" field.
8. Enter "5".
9. On the top right of the screen, click the orange "Save" button.
