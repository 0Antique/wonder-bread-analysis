Add a toothpaste to my wish list.
1. Click into the search box, located near the top right of the page and directly above the blue text "Advanced Search".
2. Type in the keyword of the desired product, in this case "toothpaste".
3. Press enter to get search results for this keyword.
4. Scroll down through the rows of search results until you find a product fitting the user request. In this case, there is one such product in the first row of results. You can tell whether a product is relevant based on the product name, which is located directly below the product's image.
5. Click the heart button for the desired product, which is located directly to the right of the "Add to Cart" button.
