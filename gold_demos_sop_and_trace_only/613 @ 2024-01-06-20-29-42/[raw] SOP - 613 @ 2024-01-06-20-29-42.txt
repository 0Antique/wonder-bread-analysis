To Post a review of my recent reading "Love story" in the r/books with my comment "I cried", do this:
1. In the header, click "forums"
2. Under that, click the button that says "Alphabetical" to see all the subreddit options.
3. Click on the "books" forum.
4. In the header, click the plus button that says "Submit".
5. In the textbox under the "title" header, enter a title of your recent reading.
6. In the textbox under the "body" header, enter your review.
7. At the bottom of the page, press the "Create submission" button.
