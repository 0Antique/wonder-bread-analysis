Delete all reviews from the scammer Arden
1. From the left menu, click on "Marketing".
2. From the popped up sub-menu, click on "All Reviews" under "User Content" section.
3. On the top right of the table, enter "Arden" in the field under the "Nickname" header.
4. Click on the grey "Search" button on the top left of the table.
5. From the list, checkmark on the first column all reviews with the Nickname "Arden".
6. On the top of the table, click on the "Actions" dropdown.
7. Select "Delete" from the dropdown.
8. Click on the grey "Submit" button next the dropdown.
9. From the popup, click on the grey "OK" button.
