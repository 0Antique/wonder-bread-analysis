Which customer(s) has completed the second most number of orders in the entire history?
1. Click on the "Reports" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "Order Total" under the "Customers" section in the third column of the menu.
3. Click on the empty box corresponding to "From:".
4. Type the earliest possible date "01/01/1923".
5. Click on the empty box corresponding to "To:".
6. Type in today's date of "12/27/2023".
7. Click the down button to the right of "Show By" to open the dropdown.
8. Click on "Year" in the dropdown menu.
9. Click on the gray "Refresh" button to search.
10. Scroll down until all results are visible.
11. Given the information, state which customer(s) has completed the second most number of orders in the entire history.
