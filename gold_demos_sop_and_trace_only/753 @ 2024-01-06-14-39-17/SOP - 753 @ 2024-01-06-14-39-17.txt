Create a private Android repository called "web_agent_android" using the right template to speed up development.
1. Click on the "plus" button at the top of the screen.
2. Click on the "New project/repository" button in the dropdown menu.
3. Click on the "Create from template" button at the right of the screen.
4. Scroll down util you find the template "Android" with the green android icon.
5. Click on the blue "Use template" button corresponding to the desired template.
6. Type the project name "web_agent_android" in the "Project name" input.
7. Click on the "Private" visibility level option.
8. Click on the "Create project" button.
