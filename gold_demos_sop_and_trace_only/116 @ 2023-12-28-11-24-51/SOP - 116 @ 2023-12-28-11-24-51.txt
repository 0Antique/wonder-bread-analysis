Show me the name of the customers who have expressed dissatisfaction with tanks products
1. Click on the "Marketing" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "All Reviews" under the "User Content" section at the bottom right.
3. Click in the empty textbox at the top of the 3rd column from right to filter by "Product".
4. Type "tank" to search for relevant reviews.
5. Click the down arrow to the left of "per page" to open the dropdown.
6. Click on "30" to ensure all results show on the page.
7. Scroll down so all reviews are shown.
8. Given the information, state the name of the customers who have expressed dissatisfaction with tanks products.
