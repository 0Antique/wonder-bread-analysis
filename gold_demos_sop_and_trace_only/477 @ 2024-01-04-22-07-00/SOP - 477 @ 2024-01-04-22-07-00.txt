Set up a new, empty repository with the name awesome_program_aided_reasoning?
1. Click the blue “Create project” button on the top right of the screen
2. Click the “Create Blank Project” pane on the top left of the middle panes
3. Click the text box under “Project Name”
4. Type “awesome_program_aided_reasoning”
5. In the black bold “Project URL” section, click the dropdown on the right side 
6. Scroll down in the dropdown until “byteblaze” is visible
7. Click the white “byteblaze” selection
8. Click the blue “Create project” at the bottom next to the white “Cancel” button
