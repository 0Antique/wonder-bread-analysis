Get the total payment amount of the last 5 completed orders
1. Click on 'SALES' on the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Orders' in the popup window.
3. Click on the 'Purchase Date' column header two times to sort orders with most recent orders first.
4. Click on the 'Filters' button on the right side.
5. Click on the status drop down box under 'Status'.
6. Click on 'Complete' from the drop down list.
7. Click on the 'Apply Filters' black button.
8. Report the sum of the 'Grand Total (Purchased)' column for the first 5 orders, which are the most recent.
