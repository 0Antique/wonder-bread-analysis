Create a private Android repository called "web_agent_android" using the right template to speed up development.
1. Click on the blue "New Project" button on the top right of the screen.
2. Click the "Create From Template" card on the screen.
3. From the list, click on the blue "Use Template" button to the right of "Android".
4. Click the textbox next to "Project name".
5. Enter "web_agent_android".
6. Click the dropdown next to "Project URL".
7. Select "ByteBlaze" from the dropdown.
8. Click the "Private" radio button under the "Visibility Level" section.
9. Click on the blue "Create Project" button at the bottom of the form.
