List the name and number of commits of the top 3 contributors to metaseq repo, ranked by the number of commits?
1. In the "Projects" page, click on the textbox which says "Filter by name" in the right side of the header.
2. In the textbox, enter the name of the project you wish to view contributions for: in this case, it is "metaseq".
3. Click on the matching project.
4. Under the project name, click on "Commits"
5. On the "commits" page, on the left hand side there is a menu bar. Click on the "Contributors" tab.
6. The Contributors are listed in order from most number of commits to least. You can see the contributors and their number of commits listed, in this case its: Susan Zhang: 70, Stephen Roller: 51, Peter Albert: 12
