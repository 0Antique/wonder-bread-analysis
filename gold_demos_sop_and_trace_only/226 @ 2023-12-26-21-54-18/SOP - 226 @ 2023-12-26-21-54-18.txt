What is the price range for products from Amazon basic?
1. Click on "Advanced Search".
2. Click on the empty text box for "Product Name".
3. Type in "Amazon basic".
4. Click the blue "Search" button below.
5. Click on option for "Sort by" to open the dropdown menu.
6. Select "Price" from the dropdown menu.
7. Record the lowest price for an item from "Amazon basic", then click the arrow next to the "Sort by" button to "Set Descending Direction" for price.
8. From the collected information, report the price range for products from Amazon basic.
