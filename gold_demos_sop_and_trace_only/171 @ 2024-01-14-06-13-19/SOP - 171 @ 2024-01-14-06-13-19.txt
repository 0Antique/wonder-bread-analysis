Tell me the full names of the repositories where I made contributions and they got less than 5 stars?
1. Click the my account button, which is an icon of the user’s profile picture, and is the leftmost icon in the purple navigation area near the top of the page.
2. Click on the user’s name, which is the bolded text at the top of the pop-up menu.
3. Click on the gray text “Contributed projects”, located near the middle of the page.
4. Scroll through all the projects that the user has contributed. You can tell if a user is a primary contributor by the role tag directly to the right of each repository name. Note which repositories have received less than 5 stars.
5. Scroll to the top of the page.
6. Click on the gray text “Personal projects”, located near the middle of the page.
7. Scroll down through all the projects that the user has contributed to. You can tell if a user is a primary contributor by the role tag directly to the right of each repository name. Note which repositories have received less than 5 stars.
8. Click the “Next” button near the bottom of the page.
9. Scroll through all the projects that the user has contributed to. You can tell if a user is a primary contributor by the role tag directly to the right of each repository name. Note which repositories have received less than 5 stars.
10. Return the projects that have less than 5 stars for which the user was a contributor.
