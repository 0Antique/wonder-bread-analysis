Submit a merge request for a11yproject.com/redesign branch to be merged into markdown-figure-block branch, assign myself as the reviewer
1. In the top search bar, enter "a11project".
2. Click on the respository which appears in the search result.
3. From the left bar, click on "Merge Requests".
4. On the top right of the page, click on "New merge request".
5. Click on the "Select source branch" dropdown.
6. In the dropdown, search for the branch name saved before, "redesign" and click it.
7. Click on the right dropdown of the "Target Branch" section.
8. In the dropdown, search for "markdown-figure-block" and click it.
9. Click the blue "Compare branches and continue" button on the bottom of the form.
10. Click on the "reviewer" dropdown.
11. Select my username, "ByteBlaze".
12. Click the blue "Create merge request" button at the bottom of the form.
