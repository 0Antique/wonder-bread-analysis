Change my reddit bio to "Freelance Web Developer".
1. In the red ribbon at the top of the page, click on your username (next to the person icon).
2. Click "Profile" in the dropdown.
3. Click the gray "Edit biography" button on the right of the page.
4. Triple click the text in the Biography box to highlight it.
5. Replace the text with the updated bio "Freelance Web Developer".
6. Click the orange "Save" button at the bottom of the page.
