What are the top-2 best-selling product in 2022
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Bestsellers' under 'Products'.
3. Click on the dropdown button next to 'Period'.
4. Click on 'Year' from the dropdown list.
5. Write the date '1/1/22' in the 'From' text box to put the start date of 2022.
6. Write the date '12/31/22' in the 'To' text box to put the end date of 2022.
7. Click on the 'Show Report' orange button on the top right.
8. Report the first 2 products listed, which correspond to the top-2 best selling products
