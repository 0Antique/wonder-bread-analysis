Presents the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format
1. On the left bar, click on "Reports".
2. Under the "Customers" sub-menu, click on "Order Count".
3. In the 'From' field, enter the first date of May 2022, which is 05/01/2022.
4. In the 'To' field, enter the last date of December 2022, which is 12/31/2022.
5. Click the dropdown next to the "Show by" field.
6. Click on "Month"
7. Click the "Refresh" button next to the filters.
8. From the given list, count and report the count of completed orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format.
