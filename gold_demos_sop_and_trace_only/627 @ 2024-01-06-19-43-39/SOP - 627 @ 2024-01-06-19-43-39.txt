Create a discussion post about "Harry Potter movie series" in a relevant subreddit and ask users for their opinions with the simple prompt, "your opinion"
1. In the header, click "forums"
2. Under that, click the button that says "Alphabetical"
3. Click on a relevant forum. In this case, I chose "movies"
4. In the header, click the plus arrow.
5. Enter "Harry Potter movie series" in the textbox under "title"
6. In the textbox under "body", type in the prompt "your opinion"
7. At the bottom of the page, press the "Create submission" button.
