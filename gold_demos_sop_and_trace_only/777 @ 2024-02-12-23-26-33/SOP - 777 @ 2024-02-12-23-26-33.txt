Reduce the price of green Hollister backyard sweater in all size by $5
1. On the left menu, click on the "Catalog" button.
2. Click on "Products" out of the available options.
3. Click on the search bar at the top left of the screen to focus it.
4. Enter "Hollister backyard" into the textbox.
5. Click on "Enter" on the keyboard.
6. On the top right of the table, click on the "Filters" button.
7. In the "Name" textbox, enter "green" as our filtering color.
8. Click on "Apply Filters" at the bottom right of the filter menu.
9. Click on the dropdown in the first column of the header of the table.
10. Select "Select all".
11. Click on the "Actions" dropdown menu above the table
12. Select "Update Attributes".
13. Scroll down to the "Price" field.
14. Under "Price", check the "Change" checkbox to focus the textbox.
15. In the "Price" field, enter "47", which is the original price reduced by $5.
16. Click on the rectangular orange "Save" button on the upper right of the page.
