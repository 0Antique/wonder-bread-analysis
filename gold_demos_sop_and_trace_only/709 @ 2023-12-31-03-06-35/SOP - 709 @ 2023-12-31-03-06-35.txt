Create an orders report from beginning of May 2021 to end of March 2022
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Reports" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Under the "Sales" area, click on the "Orders" button to reveal the "Orders Report" page
4. Locate and click on the "From" textbox and type in the date: "05/01/2021"
5. Locate and click on the "To" textbox and type in the date: "03/31/2022"
6. Click on the orange "Show Report" button in the top right
7. Scroll down to reveal the sales order report
