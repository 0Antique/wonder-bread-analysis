What is the price range for products from Amazon basic?
1. Click on 'Advanced Search' below the search box in the top right.
2. Enter 'amazon basics' in the 'Product Name' text box.
3. Click on the 'Search' blue button.
4. Click on the dropdown box next to 'Sort By' in the right.
5. Click on 'Price' from the dropdown list.
6. Note the least price of the Amazon Basics product, i.e. the price of the first product.
7. Click on the arrow button next to the dropdown box next to 'Sort By' to find the highest price.
8. Note the highest price of the Amazon Basics product, i.e. the price of the second product (as the first product is not of Amazon Basics).
9. Report the lowest and highest prices noted.
