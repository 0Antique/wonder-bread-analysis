Tell me when I last ordered my conditioner?
1. Click on the blue "My Account" button on the top right of the page.
2. On the left gray sidebar menu, click on "My Orders".
3. Click on the right arrow at the bottom of the page to see more orders.
4. Click on the row with date "1/16/23".
5. Scroll down on the page until you see a conditioner.
6. The date on this page is when you last ordered the item.
