Open my latest updated issue that has keyword 'homepage content' in its title to check if it is closed
1. Click on the rectangular icon, for Issues, right next to the '+' icon in the top navigation bar.
2. Click on the 'All' tab, under 'Issues' to list all issues.
3. Click on the 'x' icon next to 'Byte Blaze' in the search bar to remove the assignee filter.
4. Type 'homepage content' in the search bar and hit the 'return' key to filter issues having 'homepage content' in their title.
5. Click on the second issue as it has 'homepage content' in its title and it is the latest updated issue (the first issue doesn't have homepage content in its title).
6. Observe that there's a blue icon 'Closed' at the top which indicates the issue is closed.
