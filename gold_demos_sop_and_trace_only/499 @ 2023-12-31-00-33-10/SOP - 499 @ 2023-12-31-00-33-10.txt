Update order #304 with the USPS tracking number 13849373987.
1. Click on the "SALES" option in the left side bar under the "DASHBOARD" option.
2. Click on the "Orders" option in the "Sales" menu that appeared.
3. Type "000000304" in the "Search by keyword" search bar.
4. Click on the magnifying glass in the "Search by keyword" search bar.
5. Click on the blue "View" link under the column "Action" corresponding to the "000000304" order.
6. Click on the gray "Ship" button at the left of the orange "Edit" button.
7. Scroll down until you see the gray "Add Tracking Number" button in the "Shipping Information" section at the right of the screen.
8. Click on the gray "Add Tracking Number" button in the "Shipping Information" section at the right of the screen.
9. Click on the white "Carrier" dropdown meny in the "Shipping information" section.
10. Click on "United States Postal Service" option of the "Carrier" dropdown menu.
11. Type "13849373987" in the "Number" text field in the "Shipping Information" section.
12. Scroll down until you see the orange "Submit Shipment" at the bottom right of the screen.
13. Click on the orange "Submit Shipment" at the bottom right of the screen.
