Make all rocco gym tank as out of stock
1. Click on "Catalog" located on the left side gray bar
2. Click on "Products"  from the submenu
3. Type in "rocco gym tank" in the search box 
4. Press enter. Check “Select all”
5. From the “actions” drop down menu, select “update attributes”
6. Scroll down to "Stock Status"
7. Click the dropdown by "Stock Status" and select the option "Out of Stock"
