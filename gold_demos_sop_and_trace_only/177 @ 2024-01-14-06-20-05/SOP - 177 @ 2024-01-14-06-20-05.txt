Open my latest updated issue that has keyword "homepage content" in its title to check if it is closed
1. Click on the issues button, which is an icon directly to the right of the create new button.
2. Click on the tab heading “All”, located above the search bar.
3. Click on the sorting criteria button, located to the right of the search bar.
4. Click “Updated date” in the pop-up menu.
5. Click into the search bar, located above the rows of issues.
6. Type in the desired keyword.
7. Press enter.
8. Click on the most recent search result with the key word in its title. Since results are sorted recent to old, this is the first relevant search result.
9. Return whether it is closed. You can tell if it’s closed based on the open or closed tags, located above the issue title.
