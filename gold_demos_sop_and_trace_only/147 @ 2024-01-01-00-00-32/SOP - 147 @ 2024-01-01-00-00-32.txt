What is the size configuration of the picture frame I bought 2022?
1. Click on the blue "My Account" button on the top right of the page.
2. On the left gray sidebar menu, click on "My Orders".
3. Click the right arrow at the bottom of the table two times.
4. On the row which has a order date of "9/1/22", click on the "View Order" link.
5. Scroll down on the page to see all the items ordered.
6. See the size configuration of the picture frame in the title of the item.
