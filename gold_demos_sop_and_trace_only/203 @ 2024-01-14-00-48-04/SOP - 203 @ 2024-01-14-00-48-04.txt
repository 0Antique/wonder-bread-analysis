Get the purchase date and order id of the most recent pending order
1. Click on 'SALES' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Orders' in the popup window.
3. Click on the 'Filters' button on the right.
4. Click on the 'Status' dropdown box.
5. Click on 'Pending' in the dropdown list.
6. Click on 'Apply Filters' to filter pending orders.
7. Click on the 'Purchase Date' column headers two times to sort the orders with newest first.
8. Report the 'Purchase Date' and 'Order ID' of the first order displayed.
