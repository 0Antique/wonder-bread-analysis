How many commits did Eric make to a11yproject on 3/2?
1. Click on the "a11yproject" repository from the list of all repositories.
2. From the left menu, click on "Repository".
3. In the sub-menu under "Repository", click on "Commits".
4. Scroll down to the commit made on 3/2, if any.
5. Count the number of commits made by "Eric" on that day.
