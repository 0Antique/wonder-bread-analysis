To Make all Rocco gym tank as out of stock yourself, do this:
1. Click on the "CATALOG" option in the left side bar under the "Sales" option.
2. Click on the "Products" option in the "Catalog" menu that appeared.
3. Click the "clear all" button on the very right hand side to remove any existing filters.
4. Type "Rocco gym tank" in the "Search by keyword" search bar
5. Press "enter" to search
6. Click on the blue "Edit" link in the "Action" column at the right of the screen for the topmost item/row. 
7. Click on the dropdown menu to the right of "Stock Status". 
8. Select "Out of Stock" option
9. Click on the orange "Save" button at the top right of the screen.
