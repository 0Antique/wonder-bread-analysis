Change my reddit bio to "Freelance Web Developer"
1. Click the down arrow next to the username in the top right corner.
2. From the dropdown menu, click on "Profile" which is next to an icon of a person.
3. On the right, click "Edit biography" which is next to the pencil icon.
4. Click and select the current text in the Biography text box.
5. Type in the updated bio "Freelance Web Developer".
6. Click the orange "Save" button at the bottom.
