Get the total payment amount of the last 5 completed orders
1. On the left menu, click on "Sales".
2. From the popped up sub-menu, click on "Orders".
3. Click on the "Filters" button placed on the top-right of the table.
4. Under the "Status" section, click on the dropdown.
5. Select "Complete" from the dropdown.
6. From the displayed table, report the total payment amount of the last 5 completed orders.
