What is the top-1 best-selling product type in Jan 2023
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Bestsellers' under 'Products'.
3. Write the date '1/1/23' in the 'From' text box to put the start date of Jan 2023.
4. Write the date '1/31/23' in the 'To' text box to put the end date of Jan 2023.
5. Click on the 'Show Report' orange button on the top right.
6. Count how many products were sold for each product type over the month from the sales results.
7. Report the product type for which the total was the highest.
