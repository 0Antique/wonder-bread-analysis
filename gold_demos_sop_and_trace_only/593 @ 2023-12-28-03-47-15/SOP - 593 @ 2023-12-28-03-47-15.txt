Create a milestone for the upcoming task of merging all branches to main starting on March 15, 2044 and ending on March 30, 2044
1. Navigate to the left sidebar and click on the "Issues" button
2. In the new menu that appears below "Issues" click on the "Milestones" button
3. Click on the blue "New milestone" button in the middle of the page
4. Locate the "Title" textbox and type in "Task: Merging All Branches"
5. Below, in the "Start Date" textbox, click on it and type in the start date: "2044-03-15"
6. On the right, in the "Due Date" textbox, click on it and type in the end date: "2044-03-30"
7. At the bottom right, click the blue "Create milestone" button
