Change the page title of "Privacy Policy" page on my site to "No privacy policy is needed is this dystopian world".
1. Click on "Content" in the left side bar.
2. Click on the "Pages" option under the "Elements" header.
3. Click on the blue "Select" link corresponding to the page with "Privacy Policy" title.
4. Click on the "Edit" option shown in the dropdown menu shown under the blue "Select" link for the "404 Not Found" page.
5. Click on the "Page Title" text box and press COMMAND and DELETE to clear the text.
6. Type "No privacy policy is needed is this dystopian world" in the "Page Title" text box.
7. Click on the orange "Save" button at the top right of the screen.
