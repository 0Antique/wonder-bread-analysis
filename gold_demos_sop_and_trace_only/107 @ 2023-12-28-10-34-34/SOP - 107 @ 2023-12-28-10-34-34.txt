Presents the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format
1. Click on the "Reports" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "Order Count" under the "Customers" section in the third column of the menu.
3. Click on the empty box corresponding to "From:".
4. Type the first date of May 2022 "05/01/2022".
5. Click on the empty box corresponding to "To:".
6. Type in the last date of December 2022 "12/31/2022".
7. Click the down button to the right of "Show By" to open the dropdown.
8. Click on "Month" in the dropdown menu.
9. Click on the gray "Refresh" button to search.
10. Scroll down until all results are visible.
11. Given the information, present the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format.
