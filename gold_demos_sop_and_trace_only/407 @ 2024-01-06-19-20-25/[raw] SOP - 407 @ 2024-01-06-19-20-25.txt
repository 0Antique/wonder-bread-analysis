In order to Upvote the newest post in deeplearning subreddit yourself, do this:
1. In the header, click "forums"
2. Under that, click the button that says "Alphabetical"
3. Find the button that says "deeplearning" and click it.
4. Toward the top of the page, there's a dropdown menu that says "hot". Click it to reveal the options.
5. Click the option that says "new".
6. Each post is its own row in the forum. On the right-hand side of the topmost post, click the up-arrow.
