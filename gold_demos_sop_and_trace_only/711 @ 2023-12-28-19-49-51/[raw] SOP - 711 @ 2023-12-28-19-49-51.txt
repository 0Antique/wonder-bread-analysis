Create a shipping report from 08/05/2022 to 03/01/2023.
1. Click on the "REPORTS" option in the left side bar under the "CONTENT" option.
2. Click on the "Views" option in the "Reports" menu that appeared, under the third column "Products".
3. Type "07/05/2021" in the "From" date selector under the "Period" dropdown menu.
4. Type "05/31/2023" in the "To" date selector under the "From" date selector.
5. Click on the orange "Show Report" button at the top right of the screen.
