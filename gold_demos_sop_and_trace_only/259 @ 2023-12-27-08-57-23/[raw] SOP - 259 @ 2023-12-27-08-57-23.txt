To get the RSS feed token, do the following.
1. Click on the profile icon on the very top right of the page.
2. In the dropdown, select "Edit profile".
3. On the left sidebar, select "Access Tokens" (fifth from top).
4. Scroll down on the page until you see "Feed token".
5. On the box on the right, you'll see a bunch of asterisks. Click the eye icon to the right of this box.
6. The feed token will now be visible in the box.
