Open my latest created issue that has feature in its title to check if it is closed
1. Click the icon to the right of the plus sign icon called "Issues".
2. Click the third tab under Issues labeled "All".
3. Click the search box at the top of the page.
4. Type in the keyword "feature".
5. Press the Return/Enter key to execute the search.
6. Click the down arrow to the right of the search bar to open the sort by dropdown menu.
7. From the dropdown, click on "Created date".
8. Click the black, bold title of the first search result that includes the keyword.
9. Given the information, state if my latest created issue that has feature in its title is closed.
