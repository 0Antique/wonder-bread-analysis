Submit a merge request for a11yproject.com/redesign branch to be merged into markdown-figure-block branch, assign myself as the reviewer.
1. Type 'a11yproject' in the 'Filter by name' text box in the top bar.
2. Click on the 'a11yproject.com' repo from the search result.
3. Click on 'Merge Requests' in the left side bar.
4. Click on the 'New merge request' blue button on the top right. 
5. Click on 'Select source branch'.
6. Type 'redesign' in the text box of the popup window. 
7. Click on 'redesign' from the search result to set the source branch.
8. Click on 'main' under the 'Target branch' to set the target branch.
9. Type 'markdown' in the text box of the popup window. 
10. Click on 'feature/markdown-figure-block' to set the target branch.
11. Click on the 'Compare branches and continue' blue button.
12. Click on 'Unassigned' under 'Reviewer' to set myself as the reviewer. 
13. Select the 'Byte Blaze' user, myself, from the drop down list.
14. Scroll down until the 'Create merge request' blue button appears.
15. Click on the 'Create merge request' blue button to complete submitting the merge request.
