Lookup orders that are processing
1. From the left menu, click on "Sales".
2. In the popped up sub-menu, click on "Orders".
3. On the top right-center corner of the table, click on the grey "Filters" button.
4. From the opened list of filters, click on the "Status" dropdown.
5. Select "processing" in the dropdown.
6. On the bottom of the filters table, click on the grey "Apply Filters" button.
