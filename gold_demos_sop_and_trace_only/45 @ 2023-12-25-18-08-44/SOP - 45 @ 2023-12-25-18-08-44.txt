Check out the most recent open issues
1. On the left bar, click on "Issues".
2. On the top right corner of the table, make sure to select "Updated date" from the dropdown.
3. On the top right corner of the table, make sure to click on the sort icon with an arrow and 3 lines till it says "Sort direction : Descending".
4. From the given information, check out the most recent open issues.
