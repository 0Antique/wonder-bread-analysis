Modify the address of order #65 to 789 Pine Lane, San Francisco, CA, 94102.
1. Click on the "SALES" option in the left side bar under the "DASHBOARD" option.
2. Click on the "Orders" option in the "Sales" menu that appeared.
3. Type "000000065" in the "Search by keyword" search bar.
4. Click on the magnifying glass in the "Search by keyword" search bar.
5. Click on the blue "View" link under the column "Action" corresponding to the "000000065" order.
6. Click on the blue "Edit" link right to the "Shipping Address" bold text under "Address Information".
7. Triple click on the "Street Address" text box to select all the written text.
8. Type "789 Pine Lane" in the "Street Address" text box.
9. Scroll down until you see the "Zip/Postal Code" text box.
10. Click on the "State/Province" dropdown menu.
11. Scroll down or up until you find the "California" option in the dropdown menu shown.
12. Click on the "California" option in the dropdown menu shown.
13. Triple click on the "City" text box to select all the text written.
14. Type "San Francisco" in the "City" text box.
15. Double click on the "Zip/Postal Code" text box to select all the text written.
16. Type "94102" in the "Zip/Postal Code" text box.
17. Click on the orange "Save Order Address" button at the top right of the screen.
