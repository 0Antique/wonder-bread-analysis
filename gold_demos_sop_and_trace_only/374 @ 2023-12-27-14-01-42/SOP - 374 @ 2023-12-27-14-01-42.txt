Preview the Magento Blank theme for my shop
1. Click on the "Content" icon on the left to reveal the pop-out menu.
2. Click on "Themes" under the "Design" section at the bottom of the menu.
3. Click on the blue "View" button at the right of the table row for "Magento Blank".
4. Click on the colorful "Theme Preview Image" icon.
5. Thus, the Magento Blank theme for my shop has been previewed, as desired.
