Approve the positive reviews to display in our store.
1. Navigate to the sidebar located on the left side of the screen
2. Click on the "Marketing" section to reveal the pop-out menu
3. Click on the "Pending Reviews" button in the "User Content" section of the pop-out menu, to reveal the "Pending Reviews" page
4. Click the checkboxes on the left side of all the reviews
5. Click on the "Actions" dropdown menu, and the "Update Status" item revealing another dropdown menu to the right labeled "Status"
6. Click on the "Status" dropdown menu and select "Approved"
7. Click the gray "Submit" button on the right
