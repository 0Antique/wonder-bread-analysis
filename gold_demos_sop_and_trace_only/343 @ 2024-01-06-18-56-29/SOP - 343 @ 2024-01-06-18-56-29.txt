List all opened issues that don't have any labels
1. Click on "Issues" in the left sidebar
2. Click the search bar at the top middle of the page that says "Search or filter results..."
3. Type "label"
4. Click enter when the autocomplete result "Label" appears and is selected.
5. Type "="
6. Click enter when the autocomplete result "=" appears and is selected.
7. Type the keyword "None".
8. Click enter when the autocomplete result "None" appears and is selected.
9. Press Enter to display the requested issues.
