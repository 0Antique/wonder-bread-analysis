To see How many reviews our shop received in Apr 2023 yourself:
1. Click the "Marketing" tab from the left sidebar to open the dropdown menu.
2. Click on "All Reviews" in the bottom right of the menu that appeared (just to the right of the menu that contains "marketing".
3. Under the "Created" label in the table header, there are two textboxes on top of each other. Click the top one.
4. Type in the first date of Apr 2023 "04/01/2023", replacing any text that might be there.
5. Click the bottom textbox just below.
6. Type in the last date of Apr 2023 "04/30/2023".
7. Press the "Enter/Return" key to show all results.
8. You will see the answer near the top, where it says "{answer} records found", in this case 343
