What is the top-1 best-selling brand in Quarter 1 2022
1. Click on 'REPORTS' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'Bestsellers' under 'Products'.
3. Write the date '1/1/22' in the 'From' text box to put the start date of quarter 1 2022.
4. Write the date '3/31/22' in the 'To' text box to put the end date of quarter 1 2022.
5. Click on the 'Show Report' orange button on the top right.
6. Count how many products were sold for each brand over the quarter from the sales results.
7. Report the brand for which the total was the highest.
