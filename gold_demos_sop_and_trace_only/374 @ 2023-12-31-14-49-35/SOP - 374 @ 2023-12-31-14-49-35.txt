Preview the Magento Blank theme for my shop.
1. Click on the "Content" icon on the gray sidebar on the left side of the screen.
2. Click on "Themes" under the "Design" section at the bottom of the submenu.
3. Click on the blue "View" button for the table row which contains "Magento Blank".
4. Click on the icon to the right of the text "Theme Preview Image".
5. This is the preview of the desired theme.
