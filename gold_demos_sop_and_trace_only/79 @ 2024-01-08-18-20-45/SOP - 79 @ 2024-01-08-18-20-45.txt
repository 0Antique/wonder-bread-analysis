What is the total count of Not Approved reviews amongst all the reviews?
1. Click on 'MARKETING' in the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'All Reviews' under 'User Content'.
3. Click on the dropdown box under 'Status'.
4. Click on 'Not Approved' from the dropdown list.
5. Click on the 'Search' button to filter all Not Approved reviews.
6. Report the number of records shown right above the displayed table.
