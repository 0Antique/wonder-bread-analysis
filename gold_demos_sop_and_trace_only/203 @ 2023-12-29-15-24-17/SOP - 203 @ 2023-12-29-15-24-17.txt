Get the purchase date and order id of the most recent pending order
1. Click the "Sales" icon from the left sidebar to open the dropdown menu.
2. From the dropdown, click on the first option "Orders".
3. Click on the fourth column header to sort by "Purchase Date".
4. Click on the column header "Purchase Date" again to reverse the ordering.
5. Given the information, state the purchase date and order id of the most recent pending order.
