Add a new color option brown to the size S of Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt
1. Click on the 'Catalog' sidebar menu item to reveal the pop-out menu
2. Click on 'Products' (the first button) to reveal the 'Products Page'
3. Identify and click on the 'Search by keyword' text box, located on the left side of the screen
4. Type in the product name: 'Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt' and press 'Enter' on the keyboard
5. Identify the product called 'Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt' at the top of the page 
6. Click on the item's blue 'Edit' button to reveal the product page
7. Scroll down to locate the 'Configurations' section and click on the gray 'Edit Configurations' button on the right
8. Click on the orange 'Next' arrow on the top-right of the page
9. Uncheck the non-relevant sizes and check the only relevant size: 'S'
10. Uncheck the non-relevant colors and check the only relevant color: 'Brown'
11. Press the orange 'Next' button at the top-right of the page
12. Press the orange 'Next' button at the top-right of the page
13. Press the orange 'Generate Products' button at the top-right of the page
14. Press the orange 'Save' button at the top-right of the page
