Open an issue to report the issue of connection refused in ChatGPT.
1. Search for the correct repo by clicking on the search bar in the top left of the page and typing in "ChatGPT" and clicking on the corresponding repo
2. Locate the gray sidebar, and click on the "Issues" button
3. Identify and click on the blue "New issue" button in the top right of the page
4. Identify and click on the "Title (required)" textbox and type in "Connection Refused"
5. Click the blue "Create Issue" button at the bottom left of the page
