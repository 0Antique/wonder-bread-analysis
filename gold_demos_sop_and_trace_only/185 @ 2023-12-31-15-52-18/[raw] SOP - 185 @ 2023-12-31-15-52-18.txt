Give me the brand of the products that have 3 units left.
1. Click on the "Catalog" icon on the gray sidebar on the left side of the screen.
2. Click on "Products" in the submenu.
3. Click in the empty box under "Quantity" and to the right of "from".
4. Enter "3" in this box.
5. Click in the empty box under "Quantity" and to the right of "to".
4. Enter "3" in this box.
5. Press the gray "Apply Filters" button.
6. Note the brands (first part of the values in the "Name" column) of the table.
