To display the list of issues in the umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel repository that have labels related to BUG, do the following.
1. Click on the "Explore" button on the top gray sidebar under "Projects".
2. In the search box with the text "Filter by name", search for "AndroidSlidingUPPanel".
3. Click on the repository titled "umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel" from the displayed list of projects (third from top).
4. Click on the "Issues" button on the gray left sidebar.
5. In the search bar at the top of this page, type "BUG" and press enter.
