What is the price range of teeth grinding mouth guard in the One Stop Market?
1. Click on 'Advanced Search' under the search box on the top right.
2. Enter 'mouth guard' in the 'Product Name' search box.
3. Enter 'teeth grinding' in the 'Description' search box.
4. Click on the 'Enter' blue button.
5. Click on the 'Sort By' dropdown box.
6. Click on 'Price' in the dropdown list to sort products by price.
7. Scroll down to see the price of the first product, which gives the least price.
8. Click on the arrow button right next to the 'Sort By' dropdown box to sort products in the reverse order of prices.
9. Scroll down to see the price of the first product, which gives the highest price.
10. Report the lowest and highest prices.
