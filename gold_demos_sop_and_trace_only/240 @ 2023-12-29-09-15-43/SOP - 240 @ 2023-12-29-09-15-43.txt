I am doing a market survey for one stop market, show me the most expensive product from competative swimwear category
1. Click on the "Clothing, Shoes & Jewelry" tab on the top gray bar.
2. On the left hand sidebar, click on the "Sport Specific Clothing" button under "Category".
3. On the left hand sidebar, click on the "Competitive Swimwear" button under "Category".
4. Click the box to the right of "Sort By".
5. Select "Price" in the dropdown.
6. Click the arrow next to the "Sort By" box so that it points downward.
7. Click the first search result. This is the most expensive product in this category.
