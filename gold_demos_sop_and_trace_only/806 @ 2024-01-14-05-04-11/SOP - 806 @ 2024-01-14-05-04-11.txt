Submit a merge request for a11yproject.com/redesign branch to be merged into markdown-figure-block branch, assign myself as the reviewer
1. Click the merge requests button, which is an icon located directly to the right of the issues button.
2. Click “Review requests for you” in the pop-up menu.
3. Click on the blue drop down button, located near the top right of the page.
4. Type in the project name.
5. Press enter.
6. Click on the blue “New merge request” button.
7. Click on the “Select source branch” box, beneath and to the right of the bolded text “Source branch”.
8. Type in the branch to be merged.
9. Press enter.
10. Click on the Select target branch box, beneath and to the right of the bolded text “Target branch”.
11. Type in the branch to be merged into.
12. Press enter.
13. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
14. Click the blue “Create merge request” button.
15. Click the “Edit” button for Reviewers, located to the right of the bolded text “0 Reviewers”.
16. Type in the name of the first person to assign the issue to for review.
17. Press enter.
