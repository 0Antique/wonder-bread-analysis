Approve the positive reviews to display in our store.
1. From the left menu, click on "Marketing".
2. From the popped up sub-menu, click on "Pending Reviews" under "User Content" section.
3. From the list, checkmark on the first column all positive reviews.
4. On the top of the table, click on the "Actions" dropdown.
5. Select "Update Status" from the dropdown.
6. Click on the "Status" dropdown.
7. Select "Approved" from the dropdown.
8. Click on the grey "Submit" button next to the "Status" dropdown
