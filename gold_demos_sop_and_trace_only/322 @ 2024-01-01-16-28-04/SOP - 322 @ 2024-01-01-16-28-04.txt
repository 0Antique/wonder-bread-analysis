How much refund I should expect from my order canlled in May 2023 if I cannot get the shipping fee refunded?
1. In the top navigation bar, click the button that says "My Account"
2. On the left side, there is a menu with an option "My Orders". Click the "My Orders" button.
3. The center of the page has a list of orders and their corresponding date and status. Underneath, there are buttons to navigate to different pages of the list (like 1, 2, 3, 4, etc.). Navigate to the part of the list containing the relevant time frame, in this case "May 2023".
4. There is one order within the time frame that have the status of cancelled. To see the refund amount for the order, click its "View Order" button. 
5. You will be brought to an order summary page. Near the bottom, there will be text saying "Subbtotal" with a dollar amount next to it, in this case $350.42. Note that we want the Subtotal, not Grand Total, because we want the price without shipping costs.
