Create a new public project "web_arena" and add Abishek, Vinta as members
1. Click the blue “New Project” button on the top right
2. Click the “Create blank project” pane on the top left
3. Click the text box under the black bold text “Project name” on the top left
4. Type “web_arena”
5. Click the blue “Create Project” button on the bottom left of the screen
6. Click the “Project Information” tab on the top left in white and black
7. Click the white and black “members” tab on the top left under “Labels”
8. Click the blue “Invite members” label on the top right to the right of “Invite group” 
9. Type “Abishek”
10. Click the avatar icon from the popup pane that has that name
11. Click the text box under “Username or email address”
12. Type “Vinta”
13. Click the avatar icon from the popup pane that has that name
14. Click the blue “Invite” button on the bottom right of this popup window inside the shaded area
