Find the customer name and email with phone number 555-229-3326
1. Click on 'CUSTOMERS' on the left sidebar.
2. Click on 'All Customers' in the popup window.
3. Click on the 'Filters' button on the right side.
4. Enter the phone number (555) 229-3326 in the 'Phone' text box on the left.
5. Click on the 'Apply Filters' black button to filter customers with the phone number.
6. Report the name and email of the user displayed.
