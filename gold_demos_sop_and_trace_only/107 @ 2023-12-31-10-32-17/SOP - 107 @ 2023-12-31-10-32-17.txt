Presents the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format.
1. Click on the "Reports" icon on the left gray sidebar.
2. Click on "Order Count" under the "Customers" section in the submenu that appears.
3. Click on the empty box corresponding to "From:".
4. Type in "05/01/2022" to represent the start of the date range.
5. Click on the empty box corresponding to "To:".
6. Type in "12/31/2022" to represent the end of the date range.
7. Click the down button to the right of "Show By" to open the dropdown.
8. Click on "Month" in the dropdown menu.
9. Click on the gray "Refresh" button to search.
10. Given the information, present the monthly count of successful orders from May to December 2022 in MM:COUNT format.
