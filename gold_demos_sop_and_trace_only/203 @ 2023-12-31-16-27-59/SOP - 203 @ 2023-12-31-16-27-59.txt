Get the purchase date and order id of the most recent pending order.
1. Click on the "Sales" icon on the gray sidebar on the left side of the screen.
2. Click on "Orders" in the submenu.
3. Click on the "Purchase Date" column to sort by date.
4. Click on the "Purchase Date" column again to show most recent orders first.
5. Find the first row where the "Status" column says "Pending". Find the date and order ID in that row.
