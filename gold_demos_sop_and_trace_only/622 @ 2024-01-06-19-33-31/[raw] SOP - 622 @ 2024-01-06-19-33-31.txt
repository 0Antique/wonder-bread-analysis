To Ask for advice about sexual harassment in a subreddit for relations yourself, do this:
1. In the header, click "forums"
2. Under that, click the button that says "Alphabetical"
3. Click on a relevant forum. In this case, I chose "Relationship_advice"
4. In the header, click the plus arrow.
5. Enter a title that relates to the question you want to ask
6. At the bottom of the page, press the "Create submission" button.
