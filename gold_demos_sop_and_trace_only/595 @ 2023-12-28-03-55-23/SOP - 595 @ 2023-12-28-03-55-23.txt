Open the thread of a trending post on the forum "space" and subscribe.
1. Click on the "Forums" button on the red header on the top of the page
2. Click on the "Alphabetical" sub-header at the top-left of the "List of forums" page
3. Locate the "S" section denoting forums that start with the letter "S" and click on the red "space" text
4. Locate and click on the gray "Subscribe" button on the right side of the screen
