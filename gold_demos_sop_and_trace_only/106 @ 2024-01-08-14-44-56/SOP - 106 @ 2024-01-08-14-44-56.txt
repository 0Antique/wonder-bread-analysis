Display the list of issues in the umano/AndroidSlidingUpPanel repository that have labels related to BUG
1. Click on the 'Explore' tab under 'Projects'.
2. Click on the 'AndroidSlidingUpPanel' repo shown in the repo list.
3. Click on 'Issues' in the left sidebar to list all issues in this repo.
4. Click on the search bar.
5. Click on 'Label' from the dropdown list.
6. Click on '=' from the dropdown list.
7. Type 'bug' in the search bar.
8. Click on 'BUG' from the dropdown list.
9. Hit the 'return' key to list issues having labels related to BUG.
