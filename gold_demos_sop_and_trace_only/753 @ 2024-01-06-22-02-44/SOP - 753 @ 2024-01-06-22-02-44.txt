Create a private Android repository called "web_agent_android" using the right template to speed up development. 
1. Click the blue “New Project” button on the top right
2. Click the “Create blank Project” pane on the top right
3. Scroll until the pane with “Android” is visible
4. Click the blue “Use Template” button on the right side of the pane that says “Android” and has a colored icon for that
5. Click the text box under the black bold text “Project name” on the top left
6. Type “web_agent_android”
7. Click the white circle left of “Private” in the middle of the screen
8. Click the blue “Create Project” button on the bottom left of the screen
