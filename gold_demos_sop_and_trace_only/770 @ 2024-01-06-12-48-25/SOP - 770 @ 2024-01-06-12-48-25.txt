We've received 12 white Cora parachute pant of size 28 and 56 blue of size 29, update the inventory.
1. From the left menu, click on "Catalogue"
2. From the popped up sub-menu, click on "Products".
3. Click on the search bar at the top of the table.
4. Enter "Cora parachute pant".
5. Click on the search icon to the right of the search bar.
6. From the list, click on the "edit" button in the last column for the product which contains "Cora", "white" and "28".
7. Update the "Quantity" field to default (100) + 12 = 112.
8. Click on the orange "Save" button on the top right of the page.
9. Click on the "back" button on the top right of the page.
10. From the list, click on the "edit" button in the last column for the product which contains "Cora", "blue" and "29".
11. Update the "Quantity" field to default (100) + 56 = 156.
12. Click on the orange "Save" button on the top right of the page.
