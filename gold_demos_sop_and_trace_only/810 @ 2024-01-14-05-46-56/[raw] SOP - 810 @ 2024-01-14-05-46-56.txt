Based on the screenshots provided, here is a step-by-step process (SOP) for how to assign the issue regarding flash alert in the primer design guide repo to the user:

1. Click into the search bar located at the top left of the page, with a purple background.
2. Type in the key terms related to the desired repo.
3. Press enter.
4. Click on the name of the relevant search result, i.e. the name of the desired repository.
5. Click on “Issues” in the sidebar, located on the left side of the page.
6. Click into the search bar, located above the rows of search results.
7. Type in the description of the desired issue.
8. Press enter
9. Click on the first relevant search result. The title of the search result, which is the bolded text at the top left of each row, carries information on the relevance of the search result.
10. Click the “Edit” button for Assignees, located to the right of the bolded text “Assignee”.
11. Type in the name of the person to assign the issue to.
12. Press enter.
13. Click on “Issues” in the sidebar, located on the left side of the page.
14. Click into the search bar, located above the rows of search results.
15. Type in the description of the desired issue.
16. Press enter
17. Click on the next relevant search result. The title of the search result, which is the bolded text in the top left of each row, carries information on the relevance of the search result.
18. Click the “assign yourself” button for Assignees, located below and to the right of the bolded text “Assignee”.
