How many reviews our shop received in Apr 2023?
1. Click the "Marketing" tab in the gray sidebar on the left of the screen.
2. Click on "All Reviews" in the bottom right of the dropdown.
3. Under the "Created" column of the table, enter in "04/01/2023" into the first empty text box.
4. In the second empty text box under the "Created" column, enter in "04/30/2023".
5. Press enter.
6. The number of "records found" (shown near the top left of the table) is the number of reviews received in Apr 2023.
