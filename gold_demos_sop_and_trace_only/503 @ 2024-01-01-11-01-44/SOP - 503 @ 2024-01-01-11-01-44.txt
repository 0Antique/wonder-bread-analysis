Make all rocco gym tank as out of stock
1. Click the “Catalog” tab on the left side of the screen with the box icon underneath the “Sales tab 
2. Click the “Products” tab at the top of the opening tab bar on the top right, still on the left side of the screen 
3. Click the search box above the list of products
4. Backspace until the search bar is empty
5. Type “rocco gym tank”
6. Press the enter key
7. Click the first item that shows
8. Scroll down until the “Stock Status” text is visible in bold on the left of the screen underneath the blue “Advanced Inventory”
9. Click the dropdown to the right of that text
10. Click “Out of Stock”
