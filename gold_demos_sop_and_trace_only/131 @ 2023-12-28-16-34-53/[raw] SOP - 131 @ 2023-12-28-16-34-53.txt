What's the total number of items sold in the most recent 4 orders?
1. Click the "Sales" icon that looks like a dollar sign from the left sidebar.
2. From the dropdown menu, click on "Orders", which is the first option.
3. Click on the title of the fourth column "Purchase Date" to sort in Ascending order.
4. Click on the title "Purchase Date" again to reverse the sorting order from most recent to least recent.
5. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the first order.
6. Scroll down so that all information is visible.
7. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
8. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the second order.
9. Scroll down so that all information is visible.
10. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
11. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the third order.
12. Scroll down so that all information is visible.
13. Click the back arrow labeled "Back" at the top right of the page.
14. Click on the blue text that says "View" in the "Action" column for the fourth order.
15. Scroll down so that all information is visible.

18. Scroll down so that all information is visible.
