Approve the positive reviews to display in our store.
1. Click on the "MARKETING" option in the brown left side bar under the "CUSTOMERS" option.
2. Click on the "Pending Reviews" option in the "Marketing" menu shown.
3. Click on the first review with id 352 in the "Pending Reviews" table.
4. Click on the "Status" dropdown menu under the "Detailed Rating" with 4 black stars.
5. Click on the "Approved" option in the "Status" menu.
6. Click on the "Save and Next" button at the left of the orange "Save Review" button.
7. Click on the "Status" dropdown menu under the "Detailed Rating" with 5 black stars.
8. Click on the "Approved" option in the "Status" menu.
9. Click on the orange "Save Review" button.
