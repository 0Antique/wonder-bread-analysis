2. Click on the relevant project.
3. Click on the button titled "Project information" on the lefthand side menu.
4. A button called "Members" just appeared under “Project Information.” Click it. 
5. Click on the blue "Invite members" button at the right of the screen.
6. Type the username "yjlou" in the textbox that appears under the bold text "Username or email address”.
7. A dropdown of users with matching information will appear from the textbox. There should only be one option, the person you want to invite. Click on them. 
8. Click on the blue "Invite" button in the pop up window.
