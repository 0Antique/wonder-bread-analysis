Add a new color option brown to the size S of Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt
1. Click the “Catalog” tab on the left side of the screen with the box icon underneath the “Sales tab 
2. Click the “Products” tab at the top of the opening tab bar on the top right, still on the left side of the screen 
3. Click the search box above the list of products
4. Type “Phoebe”
5. Click on the blue "action" button on the rightmost column entry for the row of the product called "Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt"
6. Scroll down until the brown “Edit Configurations” button is visible on the right
7. Click the brown “Edit Configurations” button is visible on the right
8. Click the orange “Next” button on the top right
9. Click the selection box next to the text saying “Brown”
10. Click the orange “Next” button on the top right
11. Click the orange “Next” button on the top right
12. Click the orange “Generate Products” button on the top right. This will generate products in Brown of every size.
13. Scroll down to the brown table of products under “Configurations”. We will be deleting all the other Brown entries that are not in a size S.
14. Click the blue select bar next to the item Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt-XS-Brown
15. Click “Remove Product”
16. Click the blue select bar next to the item Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt-M-Brown 
17. Click “Remove Product”
18. Click the blue select bar next to the item Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt-L-Brown
19. Click “Remove Product”
20. Click the blue select bar next to the item Phoebe Zipper Sweatshirt-XL-Brown
21. Click “Remove Product”
22. Click the orange “Save” button on the top right of the screen
