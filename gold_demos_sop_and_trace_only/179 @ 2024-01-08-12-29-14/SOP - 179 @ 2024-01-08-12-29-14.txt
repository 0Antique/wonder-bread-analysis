Open my latest created issue that has 'feature' in its title to check if it is closed
1. Click on the rectangular icon, for Issues, right next to the '+' icon in the top navigation bar.
2. Click on the 'All' tab, under 'Issues' to list all issues.
3. Type 'feature' in the search bar and hit the 'return' key to filter issues having 'feature' in their title.
4. Click on the second issue as it has 'feature' in its title and it is the latest created issue (the previous issue doesn't have 'feature' in its title).
5. Observe that there's a green icon 'Open' at the top which indicates the issue is not closed.
