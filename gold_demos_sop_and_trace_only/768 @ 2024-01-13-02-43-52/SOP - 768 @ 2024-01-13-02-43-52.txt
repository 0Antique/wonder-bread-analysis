5 blue Cronus yoga pants with size 33 arrived, update the stock
1. Click on the “CATALOG” button in the dark gray column on the left hand side of the page.
2. Click on “Products” in the pop up menu, located under the heading “Catalog”.
3. Click into the search field, located above the products table.
4. Type in “Cronus yoga pant”.
5. Click on the product name of the relevant row of the products table.
6. Click into the “Quantity” field.
7. Delete the existing number.
8. Type in the value of 5 + the existing number.
9. Click the orange “Save” button, located near the top right of the page.
