Disable Karmen yoga pants from the site, they are facing some quality issues.
1. Click on "Catalog" located on the left side gray bar
2. Click on "Products"  from the submenu
3. Type in "Karmen yoga pant" in the search box above the table
4. Press enter. 
5. Check “Select all”
6. From the “actions” drop down menu, select “change status”
7. From the submenu select "disable"
