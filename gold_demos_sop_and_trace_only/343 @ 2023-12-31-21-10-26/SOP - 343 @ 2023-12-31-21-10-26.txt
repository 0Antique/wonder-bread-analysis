List all opened issues that don't have any labels.
1. Click the "Issues" tab on the left gray sidebar.
2. Click in the "Search" bar at the top of the page.
3. From the dropdown that appears, click on the fourth option "Label".
4. Click the equals sign "=".
5. Click the "None" option in the next dropdown that appears.
6. Press the enter key to show the list.
