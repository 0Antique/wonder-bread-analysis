To do I recently moved, my address is 231 Willow Way, Suite 100, Chicago, IL, 60601, update my information on OneStopShopping accordingly yourself:
1. Click on the "My account" option in the header.
2. Click the blue "Edit address" option under "Default billing address" header.
3. To the right of the "Street Address" label, there is a textbox. Replace any text here with the desired street address. 
4. In the text box right below the one you just edited, replace any text with the desires apartment/suite/house number (or leave blank if none)
5. To the right of the "State/Province" label, there is a dropdown menu. Select the correct state.
6. To the right of the "City" label, there is a textbox. Replace any text here with the new city. 
7. To the right of the "Zip/Postal Code" label, there is a textbox. Replace any text here with the new postal code. 
8. In the bottom, click the blue "Save Address" button.
