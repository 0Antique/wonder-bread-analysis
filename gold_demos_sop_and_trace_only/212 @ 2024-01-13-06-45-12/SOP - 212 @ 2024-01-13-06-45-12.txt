Find the customer name and email with phone number 555-229-3326
1. Click on the “CUSTOMERS” button in the dark gray column on the left hand side of the page.
2. Click on “All Customers” in the pop up menu, located under the bolded heading “Customers”.
3. Click into the search box, located above the customers table.
4. Type in “5552293326”.
5. Press enter.
6. Since there are no search results, we will modify our query.
7. Delete “3326”.
8. Press enter.
9. Since there are no search results, we will modify our query.
10. Delete “229”.
11. Press enter.
12. Since our search results are very broad, we will modify our query and try again.
13. Delete “555”.
14. Type in “(555)”, a common formatting of the first 3 numbers of a phone number.
15. Press enter.
16. Record the customer name and email. The customer name is in the “Name” column of the relevant row. The customer’s email is in the “Email” column of the same row.
